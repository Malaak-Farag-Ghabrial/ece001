Artificial intelligence

main page

Artificial intelligence (Al) is a wide-ranging computer science branch interested in developing smart machines that can perform tasks that usually require human intelligence. AI is a multi-approach interdisciplinary discipline, but developments in machine learning and deep learning are causing a paradigm shift in nearly every field of the tech industry.
