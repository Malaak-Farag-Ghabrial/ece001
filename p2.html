There are many abilities that contribute to artificial intelligence and without them AI wouldn't be what we expect

Ability to sense , directly correlates with object recognition. In this case, object recognition is to pick and identify objects from various inputs, such as videos and digital images. Natural Language Processing ( NLP) also contributes to sensitivity, which means the ability to read and make sense of text.

Ability To Converse which is the ability to have a conversation, is the foundation for having the ability to think. This is summed up by Predictive Analytics by predicting the probability of future outcomes based on historical data and algorithms (machine learning).

Ability to learn , requires self-improvement automatically. Such changes not only need to happen, but we also need to consider how such improvements have been made as they occur.

the ability to act, relates to thought about behavior. This is also known as "Prescriptive Analytics," and with defined criteria, it decides the best solutions / outcomes between different choices.
